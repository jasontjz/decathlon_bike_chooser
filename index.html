Hybrid Cycling

If you can't decide whether you will spend more time riding on the road and PCNs or if you might want to go somewhere a little more rugged, instead of choosing between a road bike or a mountain bike, why not choose a Hybrid bike?

What is a Hybrid Bike?
A hybrid bike as its name suggests is a mix between a road bike, mountain bike and even a city bike. The frame is designed for the rider to sit upright like on a city bike and not as aggressive as a road or even mountain bike. The tyres are wider and knobbier than a road bike for more ruggedness, yet skinnier and smoother than a mountain bike for better rolling speed on the roads. On some models, the bike is equipped with a suspension fork to smooth out bumps, yet does not bounce as much as on a mountain bike in order to achieve the perfect balance between comfort and efficiency. And while it is not equipped with a rack by default like on a city bike, the frame has the correct mounting points and Decathlon Singapore offers a wide choice of racks to choose from (available to purchase separately). It is truly a hybrid in all senses of the word, offering you the best of all the cycling worlds!

Hybrid Bicycles for the Whole Family
We have both Adult and Kids hybrid bikes available in our range. The Adult Hybrid Bikes come in different varieties and price points, offering more features like lighter frames, suspension forks, more powerful brakes and increased gearing at different price levels. The Kids Hybrid Bikes are available in 20" and 24" wheel sizes to cater to different ages and heights, starting from 6 years old all the way up to 12.

At Decathlon, we recognize this divergence and our product designers & managers have therefore heeded the call by creating two distinct sub-brands according to riding style - Triban and Van Rysel, for the endurance and racing markets respectively.

We have a range of bikes that are suitable for all skill levels - from beginners to experts.
